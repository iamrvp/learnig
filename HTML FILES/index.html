Lets Go On adventure of Gujarat

Certainly, here's a concise overview of your 5-night/6-day trip to Gujarat: Your journey commences in Ahmedabad, with visits to Sabarmati Ashram and the Calico Museum of Textiles on Day 1, followed by the Adalaj Stepwell and Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel National Memorial on Day 2. On Day 3, you'll drive to Dwarka to explore the Dwarkadhish Temple, Rukmini Temple, and Gomti Ghat. Day 4 takes you to Somnath to experience the famous Somnath Temple and nearby sites such as Bhalka Tirth and Triveni Sangam. On Day 5, you'll venture to Gir National Park for an exciting jungle safari and immerse in the natural beauty. Finally, on Day 6, return to Ahmedabad, with an optional visit to the Akshardham Temple before departing for your onward journey. This itinerary offers a well-rounded experience encompassing culture, spirituality, and wildlife, so be sure to plan accordingly, checking attraction timings and making reservations for a seamless trip.

Another adventure of Rajasthan

Embark on a captivating 5-night/6-day Rajasthan adventure, starting in Jaipur, the Pink City, where you'll explore the majestic Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal, followed by a journey to Jodhpur, the Blue City, where Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the bustling Sardar Market await. En route to Jodhpur, take in the spiritual ambiance of Pushkar with its sacred Brahma Temple and serene ghats. From Jodhpur, head to Udaipur, the City of Lakes, via Ranakpur, to admire the intricate Jain Temples. Discover Udaipur's beauty with a boat ride on Lake Pichola and visits to the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and more. This trip offers a perfect blend of culture, history, and natural splendor, allowing you to immerse yourself in Rajasthan's vibrant traditions and savor its delectable cuisine. Depart from Udaipur, carrying the colorful memories of Rajasthan with you.

Lets go to Uattrakhand

Discover the enchanting state of Uttarakhand during this 7-night, 8-day journey. Start in Dehradun, where you'll arrive at Jolly Grant Airport or Dehradun Railway Station, check into your hotel, and spend your first evening exploring local markets and Rajpur Road. The next day, explore Dehradun's attractions, including Robber's Cave, Mindrolling Monastery, and the Forest Research Institute. On day three, drive to Mussoorie, the 'Queen of Hills,' where you can visit Kempty Falls and enjoy the scenic beauty, followed by an evening stroll on Mall Road. Day four is dedicated to Mussoorie's iconic sights, such as Camel's Back Road, Gun Hill, and Lal Tibba. You'll also have time to explore the Landour Bazaar.

After Mussoorie, your journey takes you to Rishikesh, a spiritual and adventure hub. Here, visit Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, and attend the awe-inspiring Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. On day six, get your adrenaline pumping with white-water rafting, trekking, or zip-lining adventures, and don't forget to explore the serene Neelkanth Mahadev Temple. Day seven brings you to Haridwar, where you can experience the spiritual fervor at Har Ki Pauri and immerse yourself in the vibrant markets before attending the Ganga Aarti in the evening. Finally, on day eight, depart for Dehradun or Delhi, depending on your travel arrangements, marking the end of your memorable Uttarakhand journey filled with natural beauty, spirituality, adventure, and culture. Be sure to check the latest weather conditions and travel advisories and pack accordingly for a comfortable and enjoyable trip.
